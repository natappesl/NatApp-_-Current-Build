Cow

Bos Taurus

Physical Discription

Most commonly black and white, but also brown.

Behaviorial Description

Wander in groups as they graze; sometimes show curiosity towards human presence.

Location and Habitat

Found across the reserve during winter/spring

Diet and Predators

Cows eat a combination of different grasses and plants. Coyotes or mountain lions may prey on cows or calves occasionally on the reserve.

Notes

Human-introduced grazers of the VPGR, cows provide important ecosystem services such as keeping invasive grasses at bay and spreading seed of native grasses via their manure.
