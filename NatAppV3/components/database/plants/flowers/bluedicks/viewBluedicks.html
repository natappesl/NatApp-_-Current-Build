Blue dicks

Dichelostemma capitatum

Overview

The bloom period for blue dicks is between March and May. They are a very drought-tolerant plant.

Identification

Blue dicks are light-blue in color, which makes them easy to spot. They grows between 6-12 in. tall, with 2-3 leaves that are 4-15 in. long.

Habitat

Blue dicks can be found in hillsides, open woodlands, and grasslands. Some of the locations that the Bluedicks have be spotted include:

  • Tucson, Arizona
  • Malibu, California
  • Menifee, California
  • Oak Park, California
  • Palos Verdes Peninsula, California
  • Sacramento, California
  • San Jose, California
  • Gold Hill, Oregon
