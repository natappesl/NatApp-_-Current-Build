Soft blow wives

Achyrachaena mollis

Overview

Soft blow wives are an annual herb that blooms from April-May. Their flowers are very small and yellow, containing white fruits that are similar to dandelions.

Identification

Soft blow wives grow approximately one ft. in height with large, green steams.

Habitat

Soft blow wives are located primarily in the grassy plains of western North America.
