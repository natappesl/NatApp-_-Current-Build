Shadflower

Draba verna

Overview

The bloom period for shadflower is during the months of February-May.

Identification

Shadflower has four white petals with six stamen on each flower. The leaves are fairly simple with teeth at the edge of the leaf blade. The leaves grow only at the base of the plant.

Habitat

Shadflower prefers anthropogenic habitats, such as meadows and fields. It is not native to North America.
