Great Valley button celery

Overview

Great Valley button celery is a perennial species that is native to California. Its thistles offer defense against herbivory.

Identification

This plant is identified by its thistles between its purple/white sepals. Its stem is smooth and pale-green or tan in color and stands no taller than 18 in.

Habitat

This plant is typically found near vernals pools on the reserve between April and July.
